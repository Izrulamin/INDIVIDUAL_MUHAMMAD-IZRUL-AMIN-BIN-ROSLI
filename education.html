SKSG


My primary school is at Sekolah Kebangsaan Sungai Gelugor ( SKSG ) . I am really happy to have my primary school here because this school is the oldest Malay school in Malaysia ( 1826 ) . There is lot of my families who also school here such as my father , my uncle and my sister . I am really grateful to have an opportunity to enjoy the old building infrastructure before the new building are completely finish on 2013 . I can said I am the last batch that stay at old building before moving out to the new building . In addition , I still remember that all of the students especially standard 6 helped teachers to carry out things / materials / books to the new buildings . It was a great experience because all of us enjoy doing that . My result is 3A 2B.
